Project Description

Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) software has over the years proved to be an extremely beneficial and effective tool. An ERP software also takes care of another vital area of any manufacturing business – supply-chain management. In today’s age of digital transformation, more and more businesses are taking advantage of the benefits of enterprise resource planning software (ERP software). In fact, most businesses can’t afford to pass up on the automation, integration, and efficiencies offered by a fully integrated business management platform The material management module eases the entire Supply Chain Management process for any manufacturing industry.

So it is very important to construct such economically viable software which will help the small manufacturing industry as well as other sector where material management module is made in the form of e-commerce cite.

This site consists of various parts including front-end and back-end deevelopment which includes various pages for various activities such as

  • Login page
  • Inventory page
  • Form page
  • About page

We have also used recent technology like HTML,CSS and JS for front-end and Python and Django for back end services to make the site operate smoothly and efficiently.
